Jonelle Jerwick

I am a New York City-based designer with roots in rural Maryland. My work spans a range of mediums, including architecture, interiors, painting, fashion, furniture, and objects, driven by an ever-present urge to create. The result is a blend of technical precision and logic, paired with a sense of playfulness and eccentricity. I contrast structured forms with bold colors, unexpected shapes, and unconventional details.
