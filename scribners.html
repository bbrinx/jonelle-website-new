The Rounds at Scribner's

Designer: Post Company
Type: Hotel, Food and Beverage
Responsibility: Exterior and Interior Design

Location: Hunter, NY
Status: Complete


Project Description

The Rounds is a hotel project composed of a series of 11 guest cabins, and an amenity building with a bar and lounge area. The project is set in the picturesque mountains of NY and forms a relaxing space for guests to connect with nature. Local materials and makers were used to further ground the project in the context.
